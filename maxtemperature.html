City Latitude Vs. Max Temperature



This graph shows that cities that are closer to the equator (0,0) tend to have a higher temperature. The cities that are farther away from the equator are trending toward colder temperatures. Interestingly, the cities that are at 20 degree lattitude have the higest max temperature in comparison to the equator's location.

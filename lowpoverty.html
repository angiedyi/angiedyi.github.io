Low and Moderate Low Poverty Projects



This graph shows that as household average salary increases for the cities, the number of low poverty projects stay in the range between 1-10. The correlation number for this graph =0.055973582 showing that there is little to no correlation between the two factors. The two giant outliers here though are from Fremont and Sunnyvale who have high household salary averages, but have high number of projects rather than none.


This graph shows that moderate poverty projects are widely scattered in comparison to the salary ranges. Correlation = -0.197567645 which shows that there isn't that much of a connection between increasing household average salaries and it decreasing project amount in cities.

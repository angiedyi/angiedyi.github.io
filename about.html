About This Project

This WeatherAPI Project focuses on different weather elements based on random latitude and longittudes. Utilizing a random latitude and longitude generator, the Python script will choose 500 random cities from the equator source. From there, we will look at factors such as Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed.
